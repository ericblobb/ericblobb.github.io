One Thing!

If there's one thing Mum,
Just one thing!
That I think is super cool about you

Is your wisdom Mum,
Your wisdom!
That is super cool about you

You seem to know everything!
Everything there is to know
About people, places, and life in general
Out your mouth, wisdom flows

So I'm sorry Mum,
I'm sorry!
For not listening sometimes

I promise Mum,
I promise!
I'll listen every time
